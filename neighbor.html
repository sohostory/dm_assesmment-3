Plano, TX

This is my current home. I love Plano. Nice people, food culture is growing, new things are popping up constantly, beautiful parks, and so many more. We are about 20 minutes outside of Dallas. Take 75 north or Tollway. I suggest starting from Plano downtown. Then explore the parks and restaurants in the north west side.
